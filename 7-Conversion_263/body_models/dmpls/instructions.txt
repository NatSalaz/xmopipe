Download the DMPL models from the SMPL website:

1. Go to https://smpl.is.tue.mpg.de
2. Create an account or log in
3. Navigate to the "Downloads" section
4. Download "DMPLs compatible with SMPL" (dmpls.tar.xz)
5. Extract the archive and place the content in this folder.

Alternatively, the DMPL models are also available via the AMASS project:
https://amass.is.tue.mpg.de
