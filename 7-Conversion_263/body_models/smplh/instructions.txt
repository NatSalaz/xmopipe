Download the SMPL+H models from the AMASS website:

1. Go to https://mano.is.tue.mpg.de
2. Create an account or log in
3. Navigate to the "Downloads" section
4. Download "Extended SMPL+H model  (used in AMASS project)" (smplh.tar.xz)
5. Extract the archive and place the content in this folder.

Note: This model uses 16 shape parameters (instead of the original 10) and has locked head vertices.
